## Quiz (5 Questions) - Session 1

1. Define Generative AI and give an example use case.
   - **Answer:** EN AI is used to create content such as text, video, audio, image and so on. Give an input and it generates output. Example: ChatGPT
2. What is the difference between an LLM and an SLM?
   - **Answer:** Large Language Model and Small Language Model with difference in the volume of training data. 
3. Why is Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) used?
   - **Answer:** Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is used to have the AI provide answers to the specific context and to prevent hallucinations
4. Explain in your own words why “AI should be an assistant, not a replacement,” according to the instructor.
   - **Answer:** Human involvement in every step along the way, and challenging the AI with the relevant prompts to ensure that it enhances your thinking rather than      make it think for you. This is how it becomes an assistant and not replace you. 
5. Name two ways developers can use AI to be more productive.
   - **Answer:** Automate repetitive tasks, set up email agents and so on.
